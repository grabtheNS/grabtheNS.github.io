HACA原理

参考SootLib: Chemistry and Physical Models (byu.edu)HACA机理由下面六个反应构成

Reaction Rate constant
(1) C(s)-H + H ↔ C(s) . + H 2 k1=4.2×1013e−13/RT
kr1=3.9×1012e−11/RT
(2) C(s)-H + OH ↔ C(s) . + H 2O k2=1.0×1010T0.734e−1.43/RT
kr2=3.68×108T1.139e−17.1/RT
(3) C(s) . + H → C(s)-H k3=2.0×1013
(4) C(s) . + C 2H 2 → C(s)-H + H k4=8.0×107T1.56e−3.8/RT
(5) C(s) . + O 2 → 2CO + products k5=2.2×1012e−7.5/RT
(6) C(s)-H + OH → CO + products k6=0.13⋅1290T−1/2

（1）-（4）代表碳烟的表面增长，（5）（6）代表氧化
